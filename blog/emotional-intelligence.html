    What is Emotional intelligence?

    Emotional Intelligence is the ability to be able to recognise emotions in yourself and others and to then use that to guide your decisions

    What are its attributes

    Emotional intelligence has 4 main attributes:
    1. Self Management or the ability to deal with impulsiveness in a healthy
    2. Self-awareness is the ability to recognise your own emotions and know they affect you
    3. Social awareness is you empathy, your ability to understand others
    4. Relationship Management is knowing how to develop and maintain good relationships

    How is it useful?

    EQ will help you to navigate your own emotions, leading to better mental health outcome. The human skills help you properly help people and to navigate difficult and complex people situations. It can help you to set the tone of meetings, helping people digest ideas better in an appropriate way. It makes you more likeable and makes you much nicer to be around

    How can I improve?

    Mindfulness is a key component to all aspects of emotional intelligence. Being aware of how your action affect others, awareness of how you feel and how that might affect your actions and feelings towards others. Some practices include mindfulness meditation, interconnectedness exercises and counseling to develop further strategies and having someone to talk through difficult situations as they arise
